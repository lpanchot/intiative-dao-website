Core Design Principles

🧠

Domain Expertise

Informed voting by members with skin in the game and deep domain knowledge.

🏢

Entities Not Individuals

Membership tied to organizations/projects, not individuals, for stability.

✅

KYB/KYC Verified

All members undergo verification to prevent bad actors and ensure legitimacy.

🔄

Evolving Membership

New builders, tooling projects, and marketing organizations can join as they meet criteria.

dRep DAO

The governance coordination layer that sets standards, defines KPIs, and ensures accountability across all Initiative DAOs.

Governance Standards

dRep DAO establishes baseline governance standards and best practices that all Initiative DAOs follow.

KPI Definition

Sets and evolves KPI requirements (MAU, TVL, transactions) to align with Treasury priorities.

Council Elections

Elects 3 of the 7 council seats, representing dRep community interests in governance.

Vote of No Confidence

Can initiate votes to remove underperforming or misaligned council members or DAOs.

Smart Contract Treasury

Multi-Party Governance

Builder DAO Features

Domain Focus

Specialized expertise in evaluating builder products and their ecosystem impact on Cardano adoption.

Entity-Based Membership

Membership tied to organizations and live products, not individuals, ensuring consistent governance.

7-Member Council

4 seats elected by Builder DAO members, 3 seats from dRep DAO, ensuring balanced representation.

Dynamic Strategy

Funding strategies evolve based on ecosystem needs, market conditions, and KPI performance.

Open Source & Ecosystem Tooling DAO

Funds and maintains the critical infrastructure, SDKs, and developer tools that power Cardano's ecosystem.

Maintenance & Sustainability

Provides ongoing funding for critical open-source projects that require long-term maintenance and updates.

New Tooling Development

Funds development of new SDKs, libraries, and tools that expand Cardano's developer capabilities.

Domain-Specific KPIs

Tracks usage metrics for libraries, repository activity, adoption by builders, and developer ecosystem growth.

The Developer Flywheel

Better tools → More builders → More dApps → Stronger ecosystem → Better KPI metrics.
